Pictalyzur a project by Will Bush

Instagram photo classification using ANN

The pattern matching power of deep learning neural networks allows for rapid classification of photographs. At the same time, social networks are generating data at an unprecedented rate. Here we demonstrate the potential of using neural networks to predict user activities, and present an advertisement targeted to their interests.
